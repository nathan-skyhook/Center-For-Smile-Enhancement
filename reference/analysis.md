## Structural Reference — pdp.carenetic.digital

49 pages discovered. Key structural patterns for the CSE rebuild:

### Navigation Pattern

Intentionally flat — no dropdowns. 6 conversion-focused items in nav. Services are entirely absent from the nav; the homepage service grid is the sole services entry point.

Recommended for CSE: Keep services out of the nav megamenu. Nav focuses on: Schedule Appointment, About/Doctors, Patient Resources, Contact.

### Homepage Section Order (template reference)

1. Announcement banner — slim strip above nav (promotions / timely info)
2. Navigation bar — logo left, CTA-focused links right
3. Hero — headline, dual CTAs (Schedule + secondary)
4. Trust strip — insurance / quick trust signal
5. "What Sets Us Apart" — 4 feature cards
6. Patient testimonials — 3 reviews with names and quotes
7. Bridge headline — services section intro
8. Services grid — all service category cards
9. Patient Resources — 4 cards (New Patients, Forms, Insurance, Pricing)
10. Final CTA — consistent dual-button block (sitewide)

### Service Page Template (standard)

1. Hero — H1 with geo keyword, intro paragraph, 4–6 sub-service bullets, inline CTA, image
2. Sub-service anchor sections — H2 + paragraph + 4 benefit bullets (3–8 per page)
3. Smile Gallery — 3 patient images mid-page (trust break)
4. Narrative sections — practice approach, patient outcomes
5. Footer CTA — consistent dual-button block

Extended template (high-consideration: implants, veneers) adds: candidacy evaluation, process timeline, cost/financing, aftercare, "Why Choose Us" block, FAQ accordion.

### URL Patterns

- Services: flat root-level — `/cosmetic-dentistry/`, `/dental-implants/` (no `/services/` parent)
- Patient resources: `/patient-center/[resource]/`
- Scheduling: `/schedule-appointment`
- Sub-services: anchor sections within service pages, not separate URLs

### Key Structural Decisions for CSE

- Sub-services as on-page anchor sections, not separate pages (reduces URL proliferation)
- Patient Center groups all administrative/logistical content (forms, insurance, payment, FAQs)
- Footer as full-site sitemap with contact info, all service links, navigation mirrors
- Consistent dual CTA block at bottom of every page sitewide
- FAQ accordion scoped only to high-consideration service pages (implants, veneers) + standalone FAQ page
