## Target Audience

**Primary audience:** Adults 45+, fee-for-service patients seeking high-end, boutique dental care. Patients prioritizing quality, personalized service, and overall health over cost or insurance coverage.

**Secondary audience:** Existing patients needing quick access to aftercare instructions, patient forms, and appointment booking.

**What action should visitors take?** Schedule an appointment. Secondary: review services, read about the practice philosophy, and access patient resources.

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Warm, polished, and confident. Premium without being cold. Personalized and people-first. Not clinical or corporate.

**Key messages:**
1. Every patient is a VIP — a Very Individual Patient. Nothing is more important than the patient experience.
2. The practice has been infused with a Disney-inspired service philosophy since 2001.
3. Dentistry is about whole-body health, not just teeth — the mouth-body connection is central to the practice's identity.
4. The Center for Smile Enhancement is the first dental practice in the state to offer the Galleri blood test for early oral cancer detection.
5. High-touch, boutique care that exceeds expectations every time.
